## Core Continuity Notes

- Jake and Tamsin become an item in Book 1, then Jake breaks off further physical intimacy because Reina can feel enough through the bond to make consent and privacy impossible.
- Reina develops romantic/possessive feelings before she has the maturity or clean magical conditions to act on them ethically.
- Jake does not reciprocate Reina romantically until Book 3, after the bond is severed and Reina is an adult.
- The apparent trilogy goal of sending Jake home is false. The real function of the final circle is Confluence: drawing Earth and Veyr back together.
- Book 1 must look like academy fantasy with a dangerous familiar problem while quietly planting old-road, Varros-blood, guardian-kith, divine-claim, and circle-architecture clues.
